Help & FAQ

How do I use the voice assistant?

Simply say the wake word (default is "Jarvis") followed by your command. You can also click the microphone button to activate listening manually.

What commands are supported?

  • "What is the time?" - Get current time
  • "Search [query] on Google" - Perform a web search
  • "Open YouTube" - Open YouTube in new tab
  • "Tell me a joke" - Hear a random joke
  • "Torch on karo" - Simulate flashlight (visual only)
  • "Weather in [city]" - Get weather information

How do I change settings?

Click the settings icon in the top right corner to customize the assistant name, wake word, language, and more.
